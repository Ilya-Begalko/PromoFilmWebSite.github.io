After two years of searching for justice on the streets of Gotham, Batman becomes for the townspeople the personification of merciless retribution. When a series of violent attacks on high-ranking officials take place in the city, clues lead Bruce Wayne to the darkest corners of the underworld, where he meets Catwoman, the Penguin, Carmine Falcone, and the Riddler. Now under the gun is Batman himself, who will have to distinguish friend from foe and restore justice in the name of Gotham.
